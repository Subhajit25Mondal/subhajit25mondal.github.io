BookShelf: The Global Library

I am working on building a platform that can search pdf books locally as well as from other search engines.

Why BookShelf?

Introduction

In this century, there has been a rapid increase in ebooks and pdfs. Thus, people spend hours reading ebooks instead of buying an actual book. It's here where we make a bridge between users and books. So, as per our survey, people reported that it has been very difficult for them to search for ebooks and pdfs on the internet. Thus, we thought about creating an application that would help the users find the appropriate book they are looking for over the internet.

Technology Stack

Angular, Node JS, Express JS, Tensorflow JS, JSON, SQL, git
