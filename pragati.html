Pragati : Free Food for Poor People

The Pragati Project was a Startup Idea which was intended to provide free food to the poor people. I started Pragati with a goal to come with a digital approach. Thus, Pragati mainly consists of two Android apps for its operation with customers.

Problem Statement

According to a survey, around 1.3 billion people in India are sill below the poverty line. Thus, I came with a technical solution to provide food to the poor. Thus, as I belong to Computer Science Department, I tried to solve this problem through digital solution.

Solution

To solve the above problem, Me and my classmate started building a digital solution to this problem. We choose to go for the mobile app approach because most of the people use mobile devices for their day-to-day tasks. We thought of building two apps for Pragati.

First app will be for the people who will donate food to us. We thought of collecting the left over food from restaurants and houses who want to donate the left over food. If any customer has to donate the food, then he/she will have to enlist his location in the app. Our volunteers will collect the food from that location within 30min.

Another app will be for the volunteers who will work for us. The volunteers will individualy recieve location details of the pickup. They will bring the food to our facility for toxicity check. After the check, food will be donated to the poor people at Rs 1 or maybe free.

Technology Stack

Ionic-Angular, Firebase Realtime Database
